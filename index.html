How It Works

  1. Email your ticket notice, a short explanation, and any supporting photos, permits, or receipts.
  2. Jennifer reviews whether the case fits AppealDesk's current scope.
  3. If the case is a fit, AppealDesk prepares and handles the first-level dispute workflow where allowed.
  4. You only pay if the ticket is dismissed or the fine is reduced.

What We Handle

  • SFMTA parking citations
  • Current notices with enough time to respond
  • Cases with evidence such as photos, permits, receipts, or signage detail

What We Do Not Handle

  • Moving violations
  • Court-required matters
  • Private parking tickets
  • Towing or boot disputes
  • Old cases already deep in collections
  • Tickets outside the SFMTA parking workflow

What Counts As Success

  • Your ticket is dismissed
  • Your fine is reduced

AppealDesk does not bill for intake, review, filing, or a case that is still under review.

What To Send

  • A photo or PDF of the ticket notice
  • Your full name
  • Your best email and phone number
  • A short explanation of what happened
  • Any supporting photos, permits, or receipts
